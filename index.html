About Me

Welcome! My name is Crystal and I am currently a senior at the University of Pennsylvania studying Digital Media Design.

I aim to work as a software developer, and I am also open to other similar fields such as computer graphics, software design, and animation.

I was born and raised in Guam, but I currently reside in Philadelphia. Outside of school, I love to draw, sing, play the flute, and collect as much cat memorabilia as possible.

About My Major

The Digital Media Design (DMD) program is an interdisciplinary major that combines major coursework in computer graphics within the Computer & Information Science Department and Fine Arts courses from Penn's School of Design. The program is designed for students who have an interest in the computer programming, mathematics, and design behind computer graphics, animation, games, virtual reality environments, and interactive technologies.
